Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How does RenoMatch work?+

Simply tell us about your property type, budget, and renovation preferences. We'll match you with up to 5 verified interior design firms that fit your needs within 24 hours.

Is the service really free?+

Yes! RenoMatch is completely free for homeowners. We partner with interior design firms who pay us, not you. There's no hidden fees or commissions.

How are designers verified?+

All our partner designers go through strict ACRA verification. We check business registration, past projects, reviews, and financial stability before accepting them into our network.

What is the $50K Guarantee?+

Our deposit protection scheme covers up to S$50,000 of your payment to the interior designer. If something goes wrong, we'll help you recover your deposit.

How long does matching take?+

Most homeowners receive their matched designer profiles within 24 hours. You'll get detailed quotes and can start comparing right away.

Can I negotiate with multiple designers?+

Absolutely! You can receive quotes from up to 5 designers and negotiate directly with each one. There's no obligation to choose any specific designer.
